when starting on a project, it is worthwhile to survey comparable projects, noting their assets and deficits in a compare and contrast style

conduct preliminary research through comparative analysis (this could mean form, content, or both)

locate and critically evaluate two (or more) projects that relate to the class project

prepare a visual presentation with your research to share with the class. presentations are three to five minutes

title your file in the following format: lastName_firstName_r1 and email the file by 10pm the night before your presentation, or bring it on a thumb drive to give glenda at the start of the class
